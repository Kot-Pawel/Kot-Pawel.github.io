Weekly Meals App - User Guide

Welcome!

Thank you for using the Weekly Meals App. Follow these steps to plan your meals effortlessly!

Getting Started

Log in using your email and password. You must log in to access the app’s features.

1. Add a New Meal

  1. Click the “Add New Meal” button on the main page.
  2. Fill out the form with:
    • Meal Name: Enter the name of your dish.
    • Ingredients: List them, separated by commas.
    • Recipe Link: Add an optional URL for the recipe.
    • Difficulty: Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  3. Click “Add Meal” to save it!

2. View All Meals

  1. Click “See All Meals” on the main page.
  2. Browse meals and see:
    • Name, ingredients, difficulty, times used, and recipe link.
  3. To delete a meal, click the trash icon and confirm.

3. Generate a Weekly Meal Plan

  1. Click “Generate Weekly Meals” on the main page.
  2. To include “Office Days”:
    • Check the “Include Office Days” box.
    • Enter the number of office days (0–5).
  3. View your 7-day plan, including office days and meal details.

Tips & Support

- Logout: Click the logout button to sign out.
- Navigation: Use the “Back to Main” button to return to the main page.
